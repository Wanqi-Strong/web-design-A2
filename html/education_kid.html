Kids Section: Where Creativity Meets Education

School Tours

Explore treasures of the national collection through engaging discussions with our expert guides on this free school tour. Focusing on dialogue, questioning, and creativity, this free tour will embolden students to look, respond, and form opinions. The tour will be tailored to the age and level of your group and is available in Irish, English, or ISL.


CG Teens Workshop

Cailleach Gallery is excited to expand the educational opportunities available at the museum with our GTeens program! Current high-school-age students can join CGTeens and participate in a variety of events and workshops led by professional artists throughout the summer, as well as create their own works which will be showcased in the museum.


Creative Challenge- Make an Engraving

The challenge, if you choose to accept it, is to make your own engraving! An engraving is a print that is made by scratching a design into a hard surface, usually metal. But, for this activity, we’re going to use a plastic milk carton instead. You'll find all the materials and instructions listed below. This activity is for children (most suitable for ages 5 and up).
