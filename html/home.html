The Irish Trio Exhibition on April 10, 2023 @ 11 am

A Fusion of Tradition and Modernity

Welcome to the Cailleach Gallery's latest exhibition, a celebration of contemporary art from some of the most exciting artists in Galway and beyond.

This show features a diverse range of styles and mediums, from vibrant abstract paintings to striking photographic prints and intricate mixed-media sculptures.

Thank you for visiting the Cailleach Gallery and we hope you enjoy the exhibition!
